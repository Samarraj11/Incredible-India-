Popular Destinations

Taj Mahal

The city of the Taj Mahal. One of the seven wonders of the world. Agra in Uttar Pradesh is India's most popular tourist destination.

Jaipur

The city of palaces and forts, interspersed with historical monuments and gardens that testify the grandeur of the Rajput Kings.

Varanasi

The city of lights. One of the oldest living inhabitation of the world, the sacred city of Varanasi or Banaras, nestled on the banks of the holy Ganges river.

Goa

The pocket-seized paradise of Goa is a traveller's dream. Sun, sea, sand, spices and seafood along with a balmy weather. Call the soul of trekkers.

Mumbai

The city of Dreams. Mumbai, the capital of Maharasthra attracts dreamers with sky-high ambitions from every corner of the country.

Manali

The magnetic town of Manali beckons to the soul of adventures, nature lovers as well as solace-seekers. Nestled in the Himalayas.
